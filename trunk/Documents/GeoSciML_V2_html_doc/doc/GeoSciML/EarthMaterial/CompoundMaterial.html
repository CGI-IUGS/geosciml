Class <<Type>> CompoundMaterial (extends EarthMaterial )

An EarthMaterial composed of particles composed of EarthMaterials, possibly including other CompoundMaterials. Includes consolidated and unconsolidated materials as well as mixtures of consolidated and unconsolidated materials.
