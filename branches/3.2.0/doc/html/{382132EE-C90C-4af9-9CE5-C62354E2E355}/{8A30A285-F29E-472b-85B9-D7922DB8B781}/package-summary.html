Package GeoSciML.Geomorphology

Author:
Version:3.2.0
Notes:The Geomorphology package describes features that comprise the shape and nature of the Earth's land surface (ie, landforms). These landforms may be created by natural Earth processes (eg, river channel, beach, moraine, mountain) or through human (anthropogenic) activity (eg, dredged channel, reclaimed land, mine waste dumps).
