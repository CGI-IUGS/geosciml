COMMENT: "[MARK] - write the non-normative content for this requirement and provide examples of correct and incorrect usage." CONTACT_ORGANISATION MUST contain <ContactPersonPrimary> or <ContactPosition>

This is to identify the Queensland Government position responsible for the custodianship and management of the service. The individual filling this role MUST have detailed content (but no necessarily technical) knowledge of the service. It is also desirable that this role is also reflected in the position description of the described position.
